The Problem We're Solving

Public Speaking Anxiety

Lack of clarity and confidence are major barriers to effective communication. Most people struggle but have nowhere to turn for help.

Common Speaking Issues

Speakers battle with filler words like "like" and "um," inconsistent tone, poor pacing, and lack of vocal clarity that undermine even the best ideas.

Existing Tools Fall Short

Current solutions provide delayed or fragmented feedback, missing the opportunity for immediate correction when it matters most.

How Verba Helps You Speak Better

Real-Time Feedback

Instant detection of filler words and pacing issues as you speak, with gentle haptic cues to help you self-correct.

Personalized Analytics

Track your progress over time with detailed insights on your speaking patterns and improvement areas.

AI-Powered Coaching

Powered by Whisper and ChatGPT API to provide tailored feedback specific to your speaking style and goals.

Mobile-First Design

Practice anywhere, anytime. Verba fits in your pocket and works seamlessly on any mobile device.

Affordable Access

At just $6/month with a planned freemium model, get professional coaching at a fraction of traditional costs.

Coach Packs Marketplace

Access specialized coaching for different scenarios: interviews, presentations, sales pitches, and more.
